Who We Are

A trusted adviser on the King's Road for over thirty-five years.

Cowan & Rutter is an independent firm of property consultants, rooted in the heart of The Royal Borough of Kensington & Chelsea. We have built our reputation on integrity, market knowledge and enduring relationships — guiding owners, investors and occupiers through every dimension of the Kensington, Chelsea, Fulham and Putney markets.

Established by design, we offer the personal attention of a small practice with the rigour and reach of a far larger one. Every instruction is handled by a principal, and every decision is made with our clients' long-term interests in mind.
