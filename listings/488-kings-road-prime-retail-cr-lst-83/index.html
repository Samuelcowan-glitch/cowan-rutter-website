Description

The premises comprise an attractive self-contained lock-up retail/showroom unit with a forecourt area, arranged over ground and basement levels. Location

**Location** The property occupies a prominent position on King's Road, one of Chelsea's most iconic and affluent retail destinations. Renowned for its mix of luxury retailers, independent boutiques, cafés, restaurants and lifestyle brands, King's Road benefits from high levels of footfall throughout the day and attracts both local residents and visitors alike. 488 King's Road is situated within a vibrant parade of established occupiers and is surrounded by an excellent range of amenities. The property is well served by public transport, with Fulham Broadway Underground Station (District Line) and Imperial Wharf Station (London Overground) both within walking distance, providing convenient access to Central London and the wider transport network. The surrounding area is home to a strong mix of retail, leisure, showroom and professional occupiers, making this an excellent opportunity for businesses seeking a prestigious Chelsea address.
