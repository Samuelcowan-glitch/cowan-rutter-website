Description

World’s End Studios is an established boutique business centre providing a collection of contemporary, fully serviced offices and showrooms in the heart of Chelsea’s creative quarter. The building combines its distinctive industrial character with modern business facilities. Each office is individually designed, benefits from excellent natural light and can be personalised to suit an occupier’s requirements. The accommodation is arranged around three attractive central courtyards, creating a bright and welcoming working environment. World’s End Studios is prominently situated at 132–134 Lots Road in Chelsea, close to Chelsea Harbour, the King’s Road and the River Thames.
