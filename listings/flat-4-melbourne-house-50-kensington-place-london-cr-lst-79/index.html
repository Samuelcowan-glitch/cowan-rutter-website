Description

Set within the highly sought-after Melbourne House, this well-proportioned two-bedroom apartment presents an excellent opportunity to acquire a home in one of Kensington's most desirable residential buildings. The property offers bright and spacious accommodation, with a generous reception room ideal for both relaxing and entertaining, a separate kitchen, two well-sized double bedrooms and two bathrooms. Large windows allow for an abundance of natural light throughout, while the practical layout provides excellent potential for both owner-occupiers and investors alike. Residents of Melbourne House benefit from lift access, a resident porter, well-maintained communal areas and off-street parking (subject to availability), all within a secure and prestigious building. Combining generous internal proportions with an exceptional location, this apartment offers the perfect balance of comfort, convenience and long-term value.

Location

Melbourne House occupies an enviable position on the quiet, tree-lined Kensington Place, in the heart of the prestigious Hillgate Village. Perfectly located between High Street Kensington and Notting Hill Gate, residents enjoy immediate access to an outstanding selection of boutique shops, renowned restaurants, cafés and everyday amenities. Excellent transport connections are available from both Notting Hill Gate and High Street Kensington Underground stations, providing Central, Circle and District line services across London. The beautiful open spaces of Kensington Gardens and Holland Park are both within easy walking distance, while the A4 offers convenient access towards Heathrow Airport and the M4. This exceptional location combines village charm with the very best of central London living.
